The Mental Load Problem

In many households, one person carries the invisible burden of remembering, planning, and coordinating all the tasks that keep a family running.

71% of women report feeling overwhelmed by household mental load
3x more mental load carried by primary caregivers
89% of families want better task distribution

Our Solution

Mental Load makes the invisible work visible and helps families create more equitable systems for sharing household responsibilities.
