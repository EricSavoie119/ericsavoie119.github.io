What InfiniteRuler Does

InfiniteRuler turns your phone's screen into a ruler by converting pixels to real-world measurements. You can measure objects by holding your phone against them, with the ruler starting from 0 at the bottom of the screen.

It's not going to replace your tape measure, but it's handy when you need a quick measurement and don't have a ruler nearby.

How It Works

Technical Approach

The app uses your device's known pixel density (PPI - pixels per inch) to calculate accurate centimeter measurements. Each iPhone model has a specific screen resolution and physical size, so we can determine how many pixels equal one centimeter on that particular device.

Under the Hood

Device Detection Identifies your iPhone model based on screen resolution
Pixel Conversion Uses hardware specifications to map screen pixels to centimeters
Infinite Scrolling Extends measurement capability beyond screen limitations
Calibration System Allows fine-tuning for maximum accuracy
